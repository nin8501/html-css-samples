Question 3:

How can i track my parcel?

We are committed towards a sustainable future! Reducing plastic packaging is one such step towards our commitment. As soon as we start packing your order we will send you a shipping confirmation email with the delivery details and a link to track your parcel online. You will also be able to track your order under ‘Orders’ when you sign in using the demo form.

Please note, that it can take some time before the tracking information is uploaded. Please note that very small and light packages might be delivered through the mailbox, and therefore cannot be tracked.
